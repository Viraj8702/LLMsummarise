Title: INQ000385719 - Email chain between Rob Orford (Chief Scientific Adviser for Health, Welsh Government), Frank Atherton (Chief Medical Officer for Wales), Tracey Cooper (Chief Executive of Public Health Wales) and colleagues, regarding PHW/WG - National assessment, dated between 10/11/2020 and 11/10/2020.

The text outlines a discussion agenda for a meeting about the COVID-19 pandemic in Wales. The agenda includes three key points: the strategy for addressing the pandemic in Wales (suppression followed by elimination or suppression to an acceptable baseline endemicity), the current situation and projections, and global experience with tackling the pandemic. 

The text also mentions that a decision needs to be made on whether to continue with local and hyper-local lockdowns or enact additional national interventions. Finally, it discusses options for what actions should be taken in the longer term.
